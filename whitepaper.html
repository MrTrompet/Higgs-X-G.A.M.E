The Problem to Solve

In today’s market—the wildest we have ever seen—many traders fall prey to manipulation and liquidation due to inefficient strategies and overwhelming noisy signals. Big players exploit the missteps of others by leveraging enormous capital to capture profits at the expense of the less informed. Moreover, in economically disadvantaged regions and poorer areas of the U.S., access to reliable and intelligent trading education is scarce. Expensive trading courses and superficial YouTube tutorials leave ordinary individuals without the guidance needed to succeed.

How We Solve the Problem

Higgs X is an advanced AI software that acts as your real-time market assistant. Accessible directly via Telegram, it provides detailed technical analyses on demand—from candlestick signal charts and dynamic support/resistance calculations to overlays highlighting golden and death cross events from converging SMAs.

Additionally, Higgs X will soon launch a no-code task automation module that empowers you to set up market analysis workflows without writing any code. For example, you can instruct Higgs to monitor specific technical indicators and send you detailed email reports at regular intervals. Complementing this is our innovative "Ox" forum—an advanced community platform where ecosystem travelers share and rate technical analyses in an X.G.A.M.E.X format. Through our custom central command module "Ox", you can select from top-tier language models (such as OpenAi, Claude, Qwen, DeepSeek) alongside our proprietary machine learning algorithm. We also provide free didactic guides for training various ML models—including convolutional neural networks—and simulation environments that recreate market conditions, allowing you to train trading agents, save the trained model, and reuse it.

Tests

We have conducted intensive tests through both backtesting on testnets and real futures trading. Our own funds and investments from trusted advisors were committed to validating these strategies. Extensive simulations were carried out using OpenAI Gym, where models were trained for days in various simulated market environments to obtain performance metrics and ensure robust risk minimization.

This dual approach—real-world advanced futures trading combined with simulated environments—has allowed us to refine our strategies, achieving a win rate of up to 75% in volatile conditions and 90% in stable markets.
